Is Your Pharmacy Ready for November 2026?

DSCSA is a U.S. law that helps keep medicine safe. It asks pharmacies and other partners to track prescription drugs so people can trust what they get.

If a pharmacy is not ready by November 27, 2026, it can face serious trouble. That can include large fines, with some violations reaching up to $250,000, along with business delays and possible enforcement action.

This assessment shows where you stand today. It asks a few simple questions and usually takes about 5 to 10 minutes to complete.
